Redesign

Project

For this project, I was tasked to redesign shirtsbymike, an e-commerce t-shirt website for the Treehouse audience.

Problem

The website appears dated and needs a refresh in branding to highlight a featured t-shirt.

Duration

November 2020 to December 2020.

Responsibilites

Sketch, wireframe, and mockup the new website.

Website Critique

Design Principles

Color

On the landing page, the colors coral and baby green are complementary. However, the colors become dull when scrolling down the landing page.

Shapes

The use of rectangles help display each t-shirt individually.

Grouping

The shirts are grouped by "t-shirts" and "tank-tops." This grouping distinguishes each style of t-shirt and makes it easier to browse the collection.

User Flow

The user flow is logical, that is, the user browses the collection, selects a t-shirt, reviews the product details, adds it to the cart, and proceeds to checkout.
